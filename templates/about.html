System Architecture

Intelligent flow from ticker input to investment decision

Complete system architecture showing: User Input → Data Collection → Global Affairs Analysis → RAG Search → AI Decision

Data Collection

When you enter a ticker symbol, the system instantly fetches comprehensive company data and 6 months of historical price data from Yahoo Finance API.

Global Affairs Analysis

AI agent analyzes current global affairs - wars, competition, trade policies, geopolitics - and summarizes how these events could specifically impact your stock.

Classic Investment Wisdom

AWS Lambda RAG system with sentence transformers searches through legendary investment books to find relevant principles that apply to your situation, complete with exact page, chapter, and book references.

AI Investment Decision

Final AI agent synthesizes ALL collected data - market fundamentals, global events, and investment literature - to generate a BUY/SELL/HOLD recommendation with target price and detailed reasoning.

Complete Transparency

Every recommendation includes full references - news articles, book excerpts with relevance scores, and reasoning chains - so you can verify and dive deeper into any source.
